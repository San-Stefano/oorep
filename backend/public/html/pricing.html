— Pricing

OOREP is free software (for details, see also the FAQ) and as such, everyone can check out its git repository, run or modify the code.

However, I have decided to offer a subscription service with OOREP in the future, which lets users access further repertories that I am not currently offering on git. Also, the subscription service is not for using the software - it is free, afterall - but for using the software installation on this very server.

Renting the server and maintaining it is extra work, and I intend to compensate this a little with a paid subscription service.

How this will look in detail and what additional services I can offer to subscribers, I cannot tell right now. I am developing this in my copious spare time and things take time. So, stay tuned!
