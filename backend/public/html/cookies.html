Privacy Policy & Cookies

We are pleased about your interest in our web site. The protection of your privacy is important to us. In the following, we will inform you in detail about how we are handling your data.

Storing of access logs

You can visit our web site without giving out any personal data. We are merely storing access meta data in so-called server logfiles, like for example the name of a requested file on the server, the date or time of this request as well the amount of data transmitted and the Internet provider the request originated from. This data will only be used to uphold the technical availability of this server as well as to improve the level of service in general. However, the data is not of a nature that it would enable us to deduce or withdraw any personal information from it, like who you are, what your name is, where you live, etc.

Use of cookies

In order to make your visit to our web site a pleasant one and to offer certain features on it, we are using so-called cookies. These are little text files, which will be saved on your computer. Some of these cookies will be deleted after you close your web browser (so-called session cookies). Others remain on your computer and enable us to recognise your browser next time you visit this site. You can configure your browser in such a way that you will be either informed about cookies that are to be stored on your computer, or disable their storing completely. In the latter case, however, certain features on this web site might not work as intended.

SSL encryption

In order to protect the integrity of your data in transmission, we are using state-of-practice data encryption techniques (e.g., SSL) over HTTPS.

Change of our privacy policy

We reserve the right to adjust this privacy policy so that it always satisfies and adheres to current legal requirements or to reflect changes in our service offering. For your renewed visit a then new privacy policy may apply.

Request stored data and possibilities for contact

According to European law, you are entitled to request personal data, belonging to you as a person, which is stored on this web site. Moreover, you have the right to request correction or deletion of such data. In case of questions about how such data may be collected or how it can be deleted, or in case you wish to withdraw consent for collection of personal data, please contact us directly via the contact section, e.g., available at the bottom of this page or in the Impressum.
