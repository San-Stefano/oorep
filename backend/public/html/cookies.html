— Cookie and privacy policy

Right now, the cookie and privacy policies are very simple: users cannot log-in to this site, register or use any paid services. This site does therefore not use cookies, trackers, Google analytics or the like. There are no advertisements on this site. This site does not know who you are, what operating system you use, where you live or what you had for breakfast today.

Selected test users, however, can login, but those have a bilateral agreement with the author of this site, me, which does not affect the public.

So, people who use this site anonymously and free, do not have to worry about being tracked or their user data in any way recorded. Should you have further questions, please see the contact link on this site or directly send me an email to: baueran@gmail.com.
